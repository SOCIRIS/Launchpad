Meet the Minds Behind SOCIRIS

A dedicated team of innovators working to revolutionize security infrastructure across Pakistan

SOCIRIS is the result of combined expertise from four passionate individuals, each bringing unique skills and perspectives to create Pakistan's first autonomous AI-powered security system.

Our team combines deep technical knowledge in cloud operations, artificial intelligence, IoT systems, and security research with a shared vision: making advanced security accessible to every Pakistani, from large enterprises to individual homes.
