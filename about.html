About SOCIRIS

Transforming Security Through Artificial Intelligence

Our Vision

To redefine security in Pakistan by making intrusion detection, prevention, and surveillance systems (IDAPSS) both commercial and accessible for everyone—from enterprises to individual users.

We envision a future where advanced AI-powered security is not a luxury, but a fundamental right accessible to all Pakistanis, protecting what matters most in their lives.

Our Mission

To leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence, IoT technologies, and real-time analytics to create an autonomous security platform that delivers:

  • Proactive threat detection and prevention
  • 24/7 intelligent monitoring
  • Affordable security for all sectors
  • Autonomous response capabilities
Our Story

The SOCIRIS Journey

From Academic Project to National Security Solution

Born from Innovation

SOCIRIS (Security Operations Center Intelligent Response & Intrusion Surveillance) began as a Final Year Project at ILMA University in 2024. What started as an academic pursuit has evolved into Pakistan's first AI-powered autonomous security platform.

The Inspiration

Inspired by the concept of Osiris—the Egyptian deity symbolizing transformation and protection—SOCIRIS represents a transformation in how Pakistan approaches security. We recognized the critical gaps in our nation's security infrastructure and set out to create a solution that would be both technologically advanced and economically accessible.

The Challenge

Pakistan faces significant security challenges across multiple sectors. Traditional security systems are often:

  • Reactive rather than proactive
  • Expensive and inaccessible to small businesses
  • Limited in coverage and capabilities
  • Dependent on manual monitoring and response

Our Solution

SOCIRIS addresses these challenges by combining artificial intelligence, IoT sensors, computer vision, and real-time analytics into a comprehensive security ecosystem that operates autonomously while remaining affordable and scalable.
