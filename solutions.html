The SOCIRIS Story

Inspired by the concept of Osiris—symbolizing transformation and protection—SOCIRIS represents Pakistan's first AI-powered autonomous security platform. What began as a Final Year Project at ILMA University has evolved into a comprehensive solution addressing the nation's critical security challenges.

Born from a desire to revolutionize how Pakistan approaches security, SOCIRIS combines IoT sensors, artificial intelligence, and real-time analytics to deliver unprecedented protection for businesses, institutions, and homes across the country.
