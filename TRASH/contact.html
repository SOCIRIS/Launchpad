FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does SOCIRIS serve?

SOCIRIS serves a wide range of sectors including government & public sector, educational institutions, corporate businesses, residential complexes, industrial facilities, healthcare, retail, and logistics & transportation.

Is SOCIRIS suitable for small businesses?

Absolutely! SOCIRIS is designed to be both economically accessible and scalable. We offer flexible pricing models for businesses of all sizes, from small enterprises to large corporations.

How does SOCIRIS compare to traditional security systems?

Unlike traditional reactive systems, SOCIRIS uses AI-powered predictive analytics to detect and prevent threats before they escalate. We offer 24/7 autonomous monitoring, sub-second response times, and up to 60% cost reduction compared to conventional systems.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support including installation assistance, training, 24/7 technical support, regular system updates, and ongoing optimization based on your evolving security needs.

Can SOCIRIS integrate with existing security systems?

Yes! SOCIRIS is designed with integration in mind. Our RESTful API and multi-protocol support allow seamless integration with existing security infrastructure, cameras, access control systems, and other third-party tools.

What is the typical implementation timeline?

Implementation timelines vary based on the scope and complexity of your project. A typical deployment ranges from 2-6 weeks for standard installations, with larger enterprise deployments requiring 2-3 months for complete rollout.
